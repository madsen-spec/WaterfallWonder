Planning notes

Choose the day by season and weather

Spring and summer

Prioritize waterfalls, Delaware Water Gap trailheads, PEEC, river-area walks, and outdoor town days.

Fall

Lean into foliage, waterfall hikes, vineyard afternoons, Stroudsburg or Milford evenings, and scenic drives.

Winter

Look at Shawnee and Camelback first, then keep indoor waterparks, cinema, museums, and the game room ready as backups.
