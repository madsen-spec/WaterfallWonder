Planning notes

How to choose the night out

Casual

Use ShawneeCraft or Stroudsburg when the group wants a relaxed plan without overcommitting the evening.

Memorable

Use Mountain View, Milford, or Frogtown when the outing itself should feel special.

Winter or rain

Prioritize reservation-aware dining, spa, cinema, Stroudsburg, or an adults-only resort plan.

FAQ

Food and date-night questions.

What brewery outing works for a relaxed plan?

ShawneeCraft is one of the most relaxed brewery-style plans from the Bushkill side because it pairs well with the Shawnee and Delaware River corridor.

What works when dinner should feel like the outing?

Look at Mountain View for an afternoon tasting-room plan, Frogtown for a more traditional dinner, or Stroudsburg when the group wants several choices close together.

Why are no menus or hours listed?

Menus, hours, reservations, entertainment, and event schedules change frequently, so this guide links out to the venue pages before guests set the night.
