Quick answer

The quick answer: Shawnee and Stroudsburg are the easiest food corridors; Sango Kura and Mountain View add personality.

For a relaxed brewery plan, start with ShawneeCraft. For something distinctive, consider Sango Kura in Delaware Water Gap. For a vineyard afternoon, look at Mountain View. For a fuller town night, use Stroudsburg or Milford, and save Mount Airy for an adults-only dinner, spa, or casino night.

FAQ

Food and date-night questions.

What is the easiest brewery outing?

ShawneeCraft is one of the easiest brewery-style plans from the Bushkill side because it pairs well with the Shawnee and Delaware River corridor.

What is the most distinctive date-night idea?

Sango Kura is distinctive because it is a sake brewery and Japanese pub experience rather than a generic brewery or winery stop.

Why are no menus or hours listed?

Menus, hours, reservations, entertainment, and event schedules change frequently, so this guide links out to official sources for live decisions.
