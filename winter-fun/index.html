Quick answer

The quick answer: check Shawnee first for close snow, then keep indoor plans ready.

Snow sports depend on weather and operator schedules. Shawnee is the closest place to check first, Camelback adds a bigger resort day, and indoor waterparks, cinema, museums, Stroudsburg, and cabin time keep the trip strong when conditions shift.

No. Those details change too often. The guide links to official operators for live schedules and availability.
