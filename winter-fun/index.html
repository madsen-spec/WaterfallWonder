FAQ

Before the group bundles up

What is the closest ski area?

Shawnee Mountain is the closest place to look first for skiing, snowboarding, lessons, and tubing.

Is winter only about skiing?

No. The strongest winter itinerary mixes snow sports with indoor waterparks, cinema, date-night dining, hot tub time, and the cabin game room.

Do you publish lift hours or tubing session times?

No. Those details change too often. The guide links to official operators for schedules and availability.
