Rainy-day rhythm

Choose one indoor outing, then come back for the game room and hot tub.

For a quieter rainy day, choose Pocono Cinema or Pocono Indian Museum. For a kid-focused day with more activity, look at Great Wolf or Kalahari. For adults, pair Stroudsburg, ShawneeCraft, Sango Kura, or Mount Airy with a slower cabin evening.

FAQ

Rainy-day planning questions.

What is the closest rainy-day option?

Pocono Indian Museum is one of the closer compact indoor outings, while Pocono Cinema is a good low-key evening plan.

What works for kids on a full rainy day?

Great Wolf and Kalahari are the biggest indoor waterpark-style backups, but day-pass and access details should be reviewed before you go.

Can a rainy day still be a good cabin day?

Yes. The house itself has the game room, window-wall living room, hot tub, decks, and comfortable places to slow down.
