Before you bring a dog

The practical pet-policy checkpoints.

These points summarize the captured booking-rule language and the house safety notes guests should review before arrival.

Approval

Share dog details first

Dogs must be approved before the stay. Use the booking process to share the number of dogs and any details WanderHome needs for prescreening.

Dogs only

No cats or other pets

Captured listing rules reviewed May 8, 2026 described dogs-only stays and said cats and other types of pets are not permitted.

Limit and fee

Verify limits and fees

The captured rules described up to 3 dogs per stay and a fee per dog. Use the live booking page because limits and fees can change.

House care

Keep dogs off furniture

The captured rules require guests to keep dogs off furniture and clean up after them in the house and yard. Damage, stains, or excessive hair may lead to charges.

Hot tub

No pets in the hot tub

Hot tub use is for guests only. The captured rules specifically exclude pets from the hot tub and require guests to replace the cover after use.

Outdoors

Respect the waterfall setting

The creek and waterfall terrain is natural and steep. Follow house guidance, avoid leaving food or trash outside, and do not treat the creek area as a dog play space.

Dog stay checklist

Make the dog plan easy before you arrive.

Pack for mess

Bring leashes, towels, bedding or a crate if your dog uses one, cleanup supplies, food, medication, and a plan for wet paws after rain or snow.

Plan breaks

Think through dog breaks before grocery runs, dinner, waterfall outings, or longer day trips so the stay does not depend on improvising after arrival.

Protect the house

Keep dogs off furniture, follow hot tub restrictions, clean up inside and outside, and avoid treating the natural creek area as a dog play space.

FAQ

Pet-friendly stay questions.

Is Waterfall Wonder pet-friendly?

Yes. Waterfall Wonder allows dogs by approval. Verify pet limits, fees, and house rules on the booking page before reserving.

How many dogs are allowed?

Captured listing rules reviewed May 8, 2026 described dogs only, up to 3 per stay, with approval and a fee per dog. Verify the live booking policy before relying on that limit.

Are cats or other pets allowed?

Captured listing rules reviewed May 8, 2026 said cats and other types of pets are not permitted. Check the booking page for any later policy changes.

Do dogs need to be approved before booking?

Yes. The captured rules describe pre-screening and approval. Share dog details during the booking process and wait for confirmation before assuming a pet is accepted.

Can dogs use the furniture or hot tub?

The captured rules require dogs to stay off furniture and say pets are not allowed in the hot tub. Guests are also responsible for cleanup inside and outside.

Where should I verify the pet policy?
