Before you bring a dog

Pet details to review before booking.

These points summarize the public booking-rule language reviewed May 17, 2026 and the house safety notes guests should read before arrival.

Approval

Share dog details first

Dogs must be approved before the stay. Use the booking process to share the number of dogs and any details WanderHome needs for prescreening.

Pet type

Do not assume other pets are allowed

The public policy language reviewed May 17, 2026 focused on dogs by approval. Ask about any non-dog pet request before booking.

Limit and fee

Confirm limits and fees

The public listing rules reviewed May 17, 2026 described up to 3 dogs per booking and a fee per dog. Use the booking page because limits and fees can change.

House care

Keep dogs off furniture

The reviewed public rules require guests to keep dogs off furniture and clean up after them in the house and yard. Damage, stains, or excessive hair may lead to charges.

Hot tub

No pets in the hot tub

Hot tub use is for guests only. The reviewed public rules exclude pets from the hot tub and require guests to replace the cover after use.

Outdoors

Respect the waterfall setting

The creek and waterfall terrain is natural and steep. Follow house guidance, avoid leaving food or trash outside, and do not treat the creek area as a dog play space.

Dog stay checklist

Sort out the dog plan before you arrive.

Pack for mess

Bring leashes, towels, bedding or a crate if your dog uses one, cleanup supplies, food, medication, and a plan for wet paws after rain or snow.

Plan breaks

Think through dog breaks before grocery runs, dinner, waterfall outings, or longer day trips so the stay does not depend on last-minute improvising.

Protect the house

Keep dogs off furniture, follow hot tub restrictions, clean up inside and outside, and avoid treating the natural creek area as a dog play space.

FAQ

Dog-friendly stay questions.

Is Waterfall Wonder pet-friendly?

Yes. Waterfall Wonder allows dogs by approval. Review pet limits, fees, and house rules on the booking page before reserving.

How many dogs are allowed?

As of the May 17, 2026 public listing review, dogs were by approval, up to 3 per booking, with a fee per dog. Review the booking policy on WanderHome before relying on that limit.

Are cats or other pets allowed?

Do not assume cats or other pets are allowed. The public policy language reviewed May 17, 2026 focused on dogs by approval, so ask about any non-dog pet request before booking.

Do dogs need to be approved before booking?

Yes. The reviewed public rules describe pre-screening and approval. Share dog details during the booking process and wait for confirmation before assuming a pet is accepted.

Can dogs use the furniture or hot tub?

The reviewed public rules require dogs to stay off furniture and say pets are not allowed in the hot tub. Guests are also responsible for cleanup inside and outside.

Where should I review the pet policy?

Use WanderHome for the calendar, booking policies, pet approval details, Saw Creek registration guidance, and house rules. If you book on Airbnb or Vrbo, review the pet details on that platform too.
