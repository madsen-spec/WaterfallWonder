How many guests can stay at Waterfall Wonder?

Waterfall Wonder is presented for groups of up to 12. Review the booking page before reserving.

How many beds and bathrooms are listed?

This site presents 2 king beds, 4 queen beds, a queen sleeper sofa, and 3 bathrooms. Four named rooms are legal bedrooms, and the Loft and Cove are open sleeping areas.

What should we review about parking?
