Start with your family's energy

Choose one thing the kids will enjoy, then protect the downtime.

Keep some margin

A family day works better with room to change course

Morning

Do one main outing, then leave the afternoon open for the hot tub, decks, or game room.

Rain

Choose a museum, cinema, indoor waterpark, or stay-in game-room plan instead of forcing a trail day.

With teens

Give them one destination day, like Kalahari, Camelback, Stroudsburg, or Shawnee in winter.

FAQ

Questions that come up with kids

What is the most manageable family outing near the house?

PEEC is one of the most manageable outdoor choices because the visit can stay short or grow around a scheduled program.

What should we do if it rains?

Use the rainy-day guide for indoor waterparks, museum ideas, cinema plans, shopping, food, and stay-in cabin time.

Do you list ticket prices?

No. Prices, day passes, and age rules change often, so every family card links to the attraction page before guests set the day.
