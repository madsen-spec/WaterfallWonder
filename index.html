The house

Room to gather. Places to slip away.

The window-wall living room is the center of the house. From there, the group can spread out to the screened deck, covered hot tub, game room, fire pit, kitchen, and quieter sleeping spaces.

It works especially well for families, friends, and couples traveling together who want shared time without spending the entire weekend in one room.

The creekside setting

The waterfall gives the house its rhythm.

Morning coffee by the windows, evenings by the fire, and slow afternoons outside all come with the sound of a natural Poconos creek and waterfall nearby.

A real setting with real terrain.

The waterfall setting includes steep natural terrain. Enjoy it from appropriate property areas and follow posted guidance and house rules; there is no maintained public path or staircase to the water.

Where is Waterfall Wonder?

Waterfall Wonder is in Bushkill, Pennsylvania, in the Poconos and Saw Creek area.

How many guests can stay?

Waterfall Wonder accommodates up to 12 guests, with 2 king beds, 4 queen beds, 1 queen sleeper sofa, and 3 bathrooms.

Dogs by approval; see the live booking page for current terms.

Saw Creek amenities can add a lot to the stay, but access is subject to community rules, registration, passes, hours, seasons, fees, and availability.

Can guests walk to the waterfall?

The property has waterfall and creek views, but the terrain is natural, steep, and not maintained as a public path or staircase. Follow guest guidance and posted safety warnings.
