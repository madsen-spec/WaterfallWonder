FAQ

Bushkill Falls and cabin-waterfall questions.

Is Waterfall Wonder located at Bushkill Falls?

No. Waterfall Wonder is a separate Poconos cabin with its own waterfall and creek views. Bushkill Falls is a public waterfall attraction nearby.

How far is Bushkill Falls from Waterfall Wonder?

Use your exact starting point in a map app before leaving. Travel time can vary with the route, weather, and road conditions.

Can guests walk down to the property waterfall?

Guests should treat the property waterfall as a view and setting, not as a public trail. Follow the house guidance for steep natural terrain.

Should guests still visit Bushkill Falls?

Yes, if the attraction is operating and fits the group. It is one of the clearest public waterfall outings in the guide because the park manages visitor trails and guest information.
